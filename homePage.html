FAQ
  • What is HackWarts?

    HackwartS is a 24-hour onsite hackathon presented by the Technum team of Shree LR Tiwari College of Engineering. It's an intercollegiate event open to B.Tech/B.E./M.Tech/M.S. students from Mumbai colleges (Batches 2025, 2026, 2027 & 2028). The event will take place from 5th October 2024, 8:00 AM to 6th October 2024, 8:00 AM.

  • How can I register?

    You can register by visiting the Registration page on the official Hackwartz website. Each team member must provide their details during the registration process. Only teams of 5 members will be accepted.

  • Is there any registration fee?

    Yes, a registration fee will be charged for teams advancing to the final onsite round after being shortlisted in the initial selection phase.

  • Can I be part of more than one team?

    No, a participant can only be a member of one team. Violating this rule will result in the immediate disqualification of the team.

  • Can team members be from different academic years?

    Yes, teams can include members from different academic years, but all members must be from the same college campus.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    All participants are required to bring a valid college ID, their laptops, and any additional hardware they may need for their projects. Meals will be provided during the event.

  • What happens if our team deviates from the rules?

    Any violation of the outlined rules, including forming cross-campus teams or a participant being in more than one team, will result in immediate disqualification of the entire team

  • Can I make changes to my team after registration?

    No changes to the team composition will be allowed once the registration is complete. Ensure all details are accurate at the time of submission.
