Stages and Timeline

Registion Starts Start: 02nd Oct 2024
End: 06th Oct 2024

Join the excitement and be part of Hackwartz! Participants from across the nation can register online to claim their spot in this thrilling competition. In this open-call phase, teams can come together and finalize their registration with ease. After registeration, teams will dive straight into Round 1, where they can showcase their innovative ideas immediately!

Time-12:00pm
Round 1 :
Vision in Slides Start: 04th Oct 2024
End: 06th Oct 2024

Teams are invited to unleash their creativity and innovation by submitting their ideas in a captivating presentation format for Round 1. This is your chance to bring your vision to life and engage the judges with your unique concepts. The PowerPoint template will be provided once your team is registered successfully.

Time-12:00pm
Shortlisting Announcement 07th Oct 2024

After rigorous evaluation of all submissions in the Ideation Round, the shortlisted teams will be announced. These teams will advance to the main competition and be invited to participate in the on-site coding round.

Time-03:00pm
Final Round Registration Start: 07th Oct 2024
End: 08th Oct 2024

Shortlisted teams will be required to complete a FInal registration process, including payment of 150INR. This will confirm their spot in the final 24-hour hackathon. Note : Teams that fail to complete this process within the given time frame will be disqualified. (Payment Link will be shared soon!)

Time-11:59pm
Coding Marathon Start: 09th Oct 2024
End: 10th Oct 2024

This is the core phase of Hackwartz, where participants will engage in a non-stop 24-hour coding session to build their projects. Conducted offline, this round will test not only coding skills but also teamwork, time management, and creativity under pressure. The event will be held at a designated venue where all teams will collaborate in person. Judges will evaluate the final submissions on innovation, technical soundness, and real-world applicability.

Time-08:00am
